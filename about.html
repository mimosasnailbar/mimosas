Mimosas and Nails prides itself on four key pillars

SIMPLICITY

Technology

"Simplicity is the essence of happiness."

Easy - You can set your booking with our app or mobile site

Save time - We are creatures of habit, let your wellness membership spoil you with weekly or bi-weekly set appointments

No wallet - approve your experience and walk out happy

PHILANTHROPY

Giving

"Kindness in giving creates love."

Helping - At Mimosas Nail Bar, when you are supporting your wellness, you are also giving back to local Women’s charities of Charlotte

Gifting - Every month, all proceeds from our raffle will go to a Charlotte Women’s foundation

Smiling - We proudly carry cruelty-free and organic options

FUN

Happiness

"Every great business is built on friendship."

Effortless - Our database knows exactly what you prefer everytime you come back

Stress-free - No waiting in line for your pampering

Be Pampered - Enjoy our multiple entertainment options from the photo-booth, selfie room, music, televisions to our world-class signature drinks

EXCELLENCE

Pride

"Our best was good for today. Tomorrow we'll do better."

Service - Our goal is to exceed all expectations by upholding the strictest quality standards

Training - We do not practice on our customers. We are masters of our craft that have honed our skills through countless hours of hard work and training

Integrity - It is our goal to always do the right thing. We must maintain and enhance our reputation by taking care of our customers

We are a family business that will fix the problems with the traditional nail salon while building friendships with our community. Mimosas and Nails will revolutionize our industry, just as Uber, Starbucks, and Amazon transformed their respective sectors. We offer wine bar and coffee shop-quality drinks, online appointment scheduling, and personalized care, Mimosas and Nails offers its customers luxury care while remaining socially-conscious. Through our four pillars: simplicity through technology, excellence in care, joy through relationships, and philanthropy in the community, Mimosas and Nails offers a unique experience to the people of Charlotte. Our goal is to truly change the Nail Salon industry for the better and give back to the community that has given us a chance at freedom and the ability to follow our dreams.
